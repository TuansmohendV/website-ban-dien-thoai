DAH SACH API DE XUAT LAM TIEP

Muc dich:
- Liet ke cac API chua co trong he thong hien tai
- Dung cho bao cao tien do, roadmap phat trien tiep theo
- Cac endpoint ben duoi la de xuat de thiet ke he thong

Quy uoc trang thai:
- Chua co: he thong hien tai chua co model, route hoac controller tuong ung
- Da co mot phan: he thong da co du lieu nen hoac mot phan logic, nhung chua tach API day du
- Can bo sung: can them route, controller, model hoac middleware de hoan thien

1. API WISHLIST (YEU THICH)

Trang thai hien tai:
- Chua co
- Chua thay model, route, controller cho wishlist

Muc dich:
- Cho phep nguoi dung luu san pham yeu thich de xem lai sau
- Cho phep nguoi dung luu lai cac mau dien thoai ho thich nhung chua muon mua ngay

Endpoint de xuat:
- GET /api/wishlist
  Lay danh sach san pham yeu thich cua nguoi dung
- POST /api/wishlist
  Them san pham vao wishlist
- DELETE /api/wishlist/:productId
  Xoa san pham khoi wishlist
- DELETE /api/wishlist
  Xoa toan bo wishlist
- GET /api/wishlist/check/:productId
  Kiem tra san pham da nam trong wishlist hay chua

2. API UPLOAD HINH ANH (CLOUDINARY/S3)

Trang thai hien tai:
- Chua co
- He thong hien tai chu yeu dang luu image bang duong dan URL trong Product, ProductVariant, User, Review

Muc dich:
- Upload anh cho san pham, avatar nguoi dung, anh review
- Thay vi dung link anh co dinh, he thong can API upload anh de quan ly tap trung
- Admin co the upload anh san pham, User co the upload avatar va anh review

Endpoint de xuat:
- POST /api/upload/single
  Upload 1 hinh anh
- POST /api/upload/multiple
  Upload nhieu hinh anh
- DELETE /api/upload
  Xoa hinh anh da upload
- POST /api/upload/avatar
  Upload avatar nguoi dung
- POST /api/upload/review-images
  Upload anh danh gia
- POST /api/admin/upload/product-images
  Upload anh san pham cho admin

Ung dung:
- Anh san pham
- Anh bien the san pham
- Avatar nguoi dung
- Anh danh gia san pham
- Co the luu anh len Cloudinary hoac Amazon S3

3. API NOTIFICATION (THONG BAO)

Trang thai hien tai:
- Chua co
- Chua thay model Notification, route hoac controller thong bao

Muc dich:
- Gui va quan ly thong bao cho nguoi dung
- Gui thong bao khi don hang thay doi trang thai hoac khi co khuyen mai moi

Endpoint de xuat:
- GET /api/notifications
  Lay danh sach thong bao
- GET /api/notifications/unread-count
  Lay so thong bao chua doc
- PATCH /api/notifications/:id/read
  Danh dau da doc 1 thong bao
- PATCH /api/notifications/read-all
  Danh dau da doc tat ca
- DELETE /api/notifications/:id
  Xoa 1 thong bao
- POST /api/admin/notifications/broadcast
  Gui thong bao hang loat tu admin
- POST /api/admin/notifications
  Tao thong bao theo user cu the

Noi dung thong bao co the gom:
- Don hang duoc xac nhan
- Don hang dang giao
- Voucher moi
- Flash sale
- Nhac nho thanh toan
- Khuyen mai theo chuong trinh marketing
- Thong bao ho tro sau ban hang

4. API PRODUCT SPECS (THONG SO KY THUAT)

Trang thai hien tai:
- Da co mot phan
- Product hien da co cac truong screen, battery, camera, ram, storage va specifications
- GET /api/products/:id hien dang tra ve thong tin san pham, nhung chua tach rieng API thong so ky thuat
- Admin CRUD san pham da cho phep nhap specifications, nhung chua co route specs rieng

Muc dich:
- Quan ly thong so ky thuat chi tiet cua san pham
- Doi voi dien thoai, nguoi dung thuong so sanh RAM, Chip, Pin, Camera, Man hinh
- Nen tach API thong so ky thuat chi tiet de phuc vu trang chi tiet va trang so sanh

Endpoint de xuat:
- GET /api/products/:id/specs
  Lay thong so ky thuat cua san pham
- POST /api/admin/products/:id/specs
  Them thong so ky thuat
- PUT /api/admin/products/:id/specs
  Cap nhat thong so ky thuat
- DELETE /api/admin/products/:id/specs/:specKey
  Xoa 1 thong so ky thuat
- GET /api/products/compare?ids=id1,id2,id3
  So sanh thong so giua nhieu san pham

Vi du thong so:
- Chip
- He dieu hanh
- Man hinh
- Camera
- Pin
- Ket noi
- RAM
- ROM
- Tan so quet
- Cong nghe sac

5. API BRAND/MANUFACTURER

Trang thai hien tai:
- Da co mot phan
- He thong da co Brand model va Product.brandRef
- Chua co route public /api/brands va chua co route admin /api/admin/brands

Muc dich:
- Quan ly hang san xuat va phuc vu bo loc frontend
- Quan ly danh sach cac hang rieng biet voi Category
- Vi du: Apple, Samsung, Xiaomi la Brand; Dien thoai, Laptop la Category

Endpoint de xuat:
- GET /api/brands
  Lay danh sach thuong hieu cho frontend
- GET /api/brands/:id
  Lay chi tiet thuong hieu
- POST /api/admin/brands
  Tao thuong hieu moi
- PUT /api/admin/brands/:id
  Cap nhat thuong hieu
- DELETE /api/admin/brands/:id
  Ngung kich hoat thuong hieu
- GET /api/brands/:id/products
  Lay danh sach san pham theo thuong hieu

Thong tin thuong hieu co the gom:
- Ten
- Slug
- Mo ta
- Logo
- Trang thai hoat dong
- Quoc gia
- Website chinh thuc

6. API STATISTICS FOR USER

Trang thai hien tai:
- Da co mot phan
- He thong da co GET /api/orders/user de xem lich su don hang
- Chua co endpoint tong hop thong ke tong chi tieu, tong so don, hoat dong gan day cho user

Muc dich:
- Hien thi thong ke mua sam cho tung nguoi dung
- Giup nguoi dung biet ho da mua bao nhieu don va tong chi tieu bao nhieu
- Tao cam giac gan ket va ca nhan hoa trai nghiem nguoi dung

Endpoint de xuat:
- GET /api/user/statistics
  Lay thong ke tong quan cua nguoi dung
- GET /api/user/orders/summary
  Lay tom tat don hang theo trang thai
- GET /api/user/spending-summary
  Lay tong chi tieu cua nguoi dung
- GET /api/user/recent-activities
  Lay hoat dong gan day
- GET /api/user/favorite-categories
  Lay danh muc mua nhieu nhat
- GET /api/user/summary
  Lay tong hop thong tin tong quan user mua sam

Noi dung thong ke:
- Tong so don hang
- Don da giao
- Don dang xu ly
- Tong chi tieu
- So lan su dung voucher
- San pham da mua gan day
- Tong so san pham da mua
- Danh muc mua nhieu nhat

7. API SOCIAL LOGIN

Trang thai hien tai:
- Da co mot phan
- User model da co authProvider voi cac gia tri local, google, facebook
- Chua co route/controller xu ly dang nhap Google/Facebook

Muc dich:
- Ho tro dang nhap bang tai khoan Google, Facebook
- Day la diem cong lon ve trai nghiem nguoi dung va tinh hien dai cua he thong

Endpoint de xuat:
- POST /api/auth/google
  Dang nhap bang Google
- POST /api/auth/facebook
  Dang nhap bang Facebook
- POST /api/auth/social-link
  Lien ket tai khoan social voi tai khoan he thong
- POST /api/auth/social-unlink
  Huy lien ket tai khoan social

Loi ich:
- Dang nhap nhanh
- Giam thao tac dang ky
- Cai thien trai nghiem nguoi dung
- De mo rong them Apple Login hoac cac nha cung cap khac sau nay

8. API FILTER NANG CAO

Trang thai hien tai:
- Da co mot phan
- GET /api/products hien da ho tro keyword, brand, category, features, storage, minPrice, maxPrice, sort, pagination
- Chua ho tro day du color, RAM, ROM, rating, pin, chip, filter metadata rieng

Muc dich:
- Bo sung bo loc nang cao cho trang danh sach san pham
- Tich hop vao GET /api/products de frontend co the ket hop nhieu dieu kien loc

Endpoint de xuat:
- GET /api/products/filters
  Lay danh sach cac bo loc co san
- GET /api/products?brand=&category=&minPrice=&maxPrice=&rating=&sort=&storage=&color=&ram=&rom=&battery=&chip=&status=
  Loc san pham theo nhieu dieu kien
- GET /api/products/filter-options
  Lay gia tri de do frontend bo loc dong

Bo loc mo rong co the gom:
- Mau sac
- Dung luong
- RAM
- Khoang gia
- Hang san xuat
- Danh muc
- Danh gia
- Tinh nang dac biet

10. API SEARCH HISTORY

Trang thai hien tai:
- Chua co
- Chua thay model SearchHistory, route hoac controller lich su tim kiem

Muc dich:
- Luu lich su tim kiem cua nguoi dung de goi y va thong ke
- Luu lai lich su tim kiem de goi y cho lan sau va ca nhan hoa tra nghiem

Endpoint de xuat:
- GET /api/search-history
  Lay lich su tim kiem cua nguoi dung
- POST /api/search-history
  Luu tu khoa tim kiem moi
- DELETE /api/search-history/:id
  Xoa 1 muc lich su tim kiem
- DELETE /api/search-history
  Xoa toan bo lich su tim kiem
- GET /api/search-history/suggestions
  Goi y tu khoa dua tren lich su tim kiem

Ung dung:
- Goi y tim kiem gan day
- Ca nhan hoa trai nghiem
- Ho tro phan tich hanh vi nguoi dung
- De xuat san pham phu hop dua tren lich su tim kiem

11. CAC CHUC NANG MO RONG TUONG UNG

Ngoai cac endpoint API, cac nhom tren con tuong ung voi nhung chuc nang mo rong can phat trien trong he thong:

- Chuc nang Yeu thich san pham
  Nguoi dung co the luu san pham ua thich de xem lai sau

- Chuc nang Upload anh tap trung
  Ho tro upload anh cho admin va user thong qua Cloudinary hoac S3

- Chuc nang Thong bao theo su kien
  Gui thong bao khi trang thai don hang thay doi hoac khi co uu dai moi

- Chuc nang Hien thi thong so ky thuat chi tiet
  Phuc vu so sanh san pham va trinh bay thong tin ky thuat ro rang

- Chuc nang Quan ly thuong hieu rieng
  Tach Brand khoi Category de quan ly chuan hon

- Chuc nang Thong ke mua sam ca nhan
  Hien thi tong quan lich su mua sam, tong chi tieu, so don hang cua user

- Chuc nang Dang nhap bang mang xa hoi
  Dang nhap nhanh bang Google/Facebook

- Chuc nang Loc san pham nang cao
  Loc theo gia, hang, RAM, ROM va cac thuoc tinh khac

- Chuc nang FAQ va Ho tro
  Tra cuu cau hoi thuong gap va gui yeu cau ho tro

- Chuc nang Luu lich su tim kiem
  Luu tu khoa da tim kiem de goi y cho lan truy cap tiep theo
